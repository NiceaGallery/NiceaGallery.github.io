Intro


Art is a creative expression of human beings to create beauty and stir the soul of feelings. Meanwhile, art galleries are an important medium in this context. Galleries are not only places for exhibitions and art events, but also act as a means of communication between artists and society through the works on display. Amri Yahya defines that art gallery is a place where artists exhibit their works and visitors give feedback.


In its historical journey, the function of art galleries has evolved. Galleries are not only places to display and exhibit art works, but also have a role as a place for collection, preservation, and sale of art works. In addition, art galleries become a center of education for artists and also act as a magnet for tourists, Many art galleries that not only exhibits art works but also organizes various educational activities.


In conclusion, art as a creative expression of human beings is accommodated by art galleries as a space to exhibit, show, and sell art works to the wider community. More than that, art galleries also play a role as a center of interaction, education, and art trade. Its role is not only in the context of art itself, but also in the development of urban economy through tourism and as a supporter of art appreciation in society.
