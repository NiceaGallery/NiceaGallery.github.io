About Me
❮ ❯


Hello! My name is Khairunnisa Rahma. My interests, talents, and hobbies are fully focused on the field of art, especially drawing and painting. Since I was kid, I've been fascinated by the beauty of the art world. When I create a piece, I feel like I'm being myself and I find an irreplaceable joy in every step of creating something meaningful. Art is not just a hobby for me, but the foundation of my identity and how I interact with the world.


Rapunzel from Disney's "The Tangled" is one of my role models because of her braveness pursuit of freedom. I am inspired by her unwavering spirit in seeking her identity beyond the restrictions that once shackled her. Although Rapunzel is confined to a tower for her entire life, her strong desire to understand what happens outside the walls of the tower reflects a passionate curiosity that manages to amaze me. Her passion to understand the unknown world shows that curiosity and determination can lead one to explore amazing new territories.


The consistent support from my family and the many exciting things I have yet to try continue to push me to grow and become a better version of myself. Both aspects provide positive energy and encouragement to navigate life with a greater spirit, opening myself to new experiences, and continuously learning from every opportunity that comes my way.
